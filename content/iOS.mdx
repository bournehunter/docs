# Boop SMS Business Cards - iOS Guide

This guide covers iOS-specific features and instructions for using Boop on your Apple device.

## Installation

1. Visit [link.boop.vip/ios](https://link.boop.vip/ios) on your iOS device
2. Download and install the Boop app
3. Open the app and tap "Sign Up" to create your account

## iOS-Specific Features

### Apple Wallet Integration

Boop allows you to add your QR code to Apple Wallet for quick access:

1. Open the Boop app
2. On the home screen, click "Add to Apple Wallet"
3. Follow the prompts to add your Boop QR code to Apple Wallet
4. To share, open Apple Wallet and display your Boop QR code
5. The recipient follows steps 3-8 from the standard QR Code Sharing method

### Save to Photos

For quick access to your QR code:

1. Open the Boop app
2. Go to Settings
3. Select the option to save your QR code to photos
4. Your QR code is now saved in your phone's gallery
5. To share, simply open your gallery and show the QR code

### Set as Wallpaper or Lock Screen

Make your QR code even more accessible:

1. Save your QR code to Photos using the steps above
2. Go to your iPhone's wallpaper or lock screen settings
3. Select the Boop QR code image
4. Set it as your wallpaper or lock screen
5. To share, simply show your lock screen or wallpaper

## iOS App Tips

- Enable notifications to stay updated on new connections and messages
- Use iOS widgets to add quick access to your Boop QR code on your home screen
- Utilize iOS's built-in QR code scanner in the camera app for easy scanning of others' Boop QR codes

Remember, all changes made in the iOS app will sync automatically with your web account, ensuring a seamless cross-platform experience.
